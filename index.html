Our Services

Individual Income Tax Return (T1)

Preparation and filing of a personal tax return (T1) must be completed annually and submitted to CRA no later than April 30th of the following year. If you have a balance owing, it must be paid by April 30th.

Bookkeeping

Depending on the size of your business, you might need a bookkeeper just a day or two a week or on a monthly basis, surf through our Bookkeeping Packages to determine which one best suits your needs.

Corporate Tax Return (T2)

The corporate income tax return is required to be filed no later than six months following the year end. If you are required to make installment payments throughout the year, they are due on March 15, June 15, September 15, and December 15.
